A landing page is a standalone web page specifically designed to convert visitors into leads or customers. It's the page a user "lands" on after clicking a link from an ad, email, or other digital source. Unlike a website's homepage, a landing page typically focuses on a single, clear call to action, such as signing up for a newsletter, downloading a resource, or making a purchase.
